Donate

Every dollar counts! We appreciate each contribution, no matter how large or small. If you are unable to make a monetary donation, but still want to help, see our Other Ways To Give page for alternative ways (including in-kind donations) to support International Spay/Neuter Network (ISNN).

ISNN is an all-volunteer organization. The veterinarians and technicians participating in our Community Spay/Neuter Clinics generously volunteer their time and pay their own travel expenses. We are also fortunate to have local hotels donate accommodations. This means all funds go directly to program expenses, the majority towards the purchase of supplies and medications required for spay/neuter surgeries.

Donations to ISNN are tax-deductible. We are an IRS 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization.

Planned Giving

A bequest is a simple and meaningful way to support ISNN in the future. In addition to the impact your gift has on our organization, there are a number of other benefits when you include ISNN in your will.

  • Receive estate tax charitable deduction.
  • Lessen the burden of taxes on your family.
  • Leave a lasting legacy.

If you are considering leaving a bequest to ISNN, we have an information packet to help guide you. Please contact us if you would like to discuss a bequest.
