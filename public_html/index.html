International Spay/Neuter Network (ISNN) is an all-volunteer, nonprofit organization working to improve the lives of Jamaica’s animals and people. Our goal is to reduce dog and cat overpopulation using cost-effective sterilization, and to improve the welfare of animals through humane education.
